About us

City Bakery is a private limited company which is registered in Kathmandu district, Tarakeshwor municipality wada number 06, Dharmasthali tole. It is a leading bakery and coffee shop of Nepal. Since its incorporation in 'Office of the Company Registrar' in 2078 B.S., City Bakery has been offering delicious bakery products, coffee in Nepal. With a vision to serve fresh and healthy bakery products to Nepalese people, City Bakery was established.

To create its own identity in Nepalese bakery industry, City Bakery was incorporated by leading entrepreneurs of Nepal. City Bakery has always maintained core policy of using quality raw material and producing higher quality goods to achieve such a goal. City Bakery believes in employing higher level of technology as well as manpower educated and skilled in the relevant field. The corporate office of City Bakery is situated in Tarakeshwor Municipality of Kathmandu district, however, it has objective to expand its network throughout the country. It has already started some outlets in Kathmandu valley and is planning to open some outlets outside Kathmandu valley in the days to come. City Bakery has divided its product and services into four categories viz. bakery products, fast food, cake and coffee.

Bakery production is a segment of City Bakery which is solely focused on healthy and fresh bakery production and supply process. It produces various consumable bakery products
